How to use this application


Enter an address and we will show you the average visitors by day for the census tract containing that address as averaged over a recent span of time. We will also show you the percentage of people that said they wear masks when they expect to be around people (Always/Frequently/Sometimes/Rarely/Never). Finally, we will return the number of people that have had confirmed cases of COVID since the start of the pandemic in the county containing your chosen destination.

Data Sources:

Location data: SafeGraph Research
Mask use survey: NYT
COVID rates by county: NYT
